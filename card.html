Bald Eagle

Bald Eagles that live in the northern climates migrate during the winter. Eagles that live in the southern climates do not migrate and stay in their home area.

  • Scientific Name: Haliaeetus leucocephalus
  • Average Length: 28-40 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: North America from Northern Canada to Northern Mexico

The Bald Eagle was once on the endangered species list as a result of DDT usage. DDT usage was banned by both the United States and Canada. The Bald Eagle has since recovered its population and is no longer on the endangered species list.
